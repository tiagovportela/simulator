Infectious Disease Simulator

This is a simple Simulator.

Here you can see, how variables like Social Distance, Recovery time, etc. affect the behavior of infectious diseases in a population.




Susceptible - Healthy people who can get infected

Infected People who have no symptoms.

Infected People who have Symptoms.

Removed - Dead + People who recovered and were immune

Quarantine - If selected, people infected with symptoms are removed from the population.
